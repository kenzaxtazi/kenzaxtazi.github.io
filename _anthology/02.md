The glow of the sunset is splendid\
Gently coloring your face\
I can only think it is beautiful

Life\
It’s not getting older\
Just\
Getting soaked in twilight

In a bookcase\
Like bright autumn leaves piled up one by one

---

석양빛이 아름답다\
곱게 물든 너의 얼굴\
예쁘기만 하다

인생은\
늙어 가는 것이 아니라\
그저\
황혼빛에 젖어가는 것이다

책장 속에\
차곡차곡 쌓아둔 단풍잎처럼
