### Strength

#### Kim Da Yeong | 2021 Citizen Contest

Can you just look at me\
I don't need any comfort\
Just watch over me\
Whether I am doing well enough\
Whether I am crying because I am tired\
If I am crying \
Please put a hand on my small shoulder once\
That's all I need
